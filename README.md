**Community Health Map** is an open-source, web-based platform designed for anonymous symptom reporting and local health alerts. Our mission is to empower communities with real-time health data while ensuring absolute privacy.

---

## 🎯 The Problem & Solution
In many communities, people have no way to know about local health trends. If many neighbors have flu symptoms, there is no alert system. 
**Our Solution:** A completely anonymous map where residents can drop a pin with their symptoms, creating a real-time heatmap of local health.
